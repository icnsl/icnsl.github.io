## Bio
<p align="justify">
I received my B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from Seoul National University in 2010, 2012, and 2017. I worked as a Staff Engineer at Samsung Electronics until October 2022, contributing to Wi-Fi firmware development and IEEE 802.11 standardization. Until February 2025, I was a Senior Researcher at the Korea Railroad Research Institute, focusing on wireless LAN/PAN systems, positioning, and mission-critical railway applications. Since March 2025, I have been an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Information Technology at Sejong University, focusing on intelligent communication and netwokred systems.
</p>
